Candy Bar of the Day

where I single out one or two of my favorite candy selections

The Most Disgusting Award had so many contestants, but I'm going to hand it to Steve "Jane-Jane Tasty Tuna Tidbits" Carlson. What do you think the main ingredient in Jane-Jane Tasty Tuna Tidbits is? Nope, it's TUNA! These tidbits come in at #4 on thetoptens.com list of Most Disgusting Candies, sandwiched between two candies made out of insects. At #3 is Crick-ettes, which come in Salt-n-Vinegar, Bacon-n-Cheddar Cheese, and Sour Cream-n-Onion flavors. At #5 is Ant Candy, which only comes in Ant.

Tasty Tuna Tidbits are also well ahead of both Gorilla Boogers and Candy Scabs. Yum!

But don't take my word for it, read this candy review excerpt from the internet, which apparently chronicles literally everything.

"There you are at the party when you suddenly see a bowl of shiny foil wrapped candies sitting in a bowl. You reach down and unwrap it while talking with that person that you have wanted to make a good impression. Continuing that conversation you pop the delicious treat into your mouth. You smile and think, “Mmm...er...Oh my goodness. I-I-I am going to hurl. This tastes like… FISH! I can’t spit this out, they will see…” You chew and swallow as soon as you can. You just ate a piece of Jane-Jane Tasty Tuna Tidbits Candy. "
