Upsets...who picked 'em

Highlighting those brave souls that chose oddball longshots to win games

The Final Four this year features an overwhelming favorite #1 seed Kentucky Wildcats, the easily-having-the-most-fun #1 seed Wisconsin Badgers, the Evil Empire #1 seed Duke, and an "upstart" #7 seed Michigan State. But is Michigan State an underdog? Not if you ask JQMCBPers. Check this out: Not only had 87 people selected this "#7 seed" to go all the way to the Final Four, but that was more than the team they beat, #4 Louisville, to get there, as well as more than than the 3, 5, and 6 seeds, including a whopping 80 more selections than #6 Providence. So yeah, #7 seed, but not really. JQMCBPers knew better.
