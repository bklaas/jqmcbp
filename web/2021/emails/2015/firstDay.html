Whahappened?

WOW, did the brackets blow up early today. In case you didn't know, there is just a wee bit of bias for Iowa State in this pool, and that's even if head coach Fred Hoiberg did end my basketball career. Well, ISU-heavy brackets are straight out the window after a surprising upset at the hands of the Alabama-Birmingham "Blazers", which I was shocked to learn is not referring to a lightweight, often striped or brightly colored sports jacket having pockets and notched lapels. Nope, the UAB Blazer is some kind of fire-breathing dragon type thing. I wish someone would have told ISU, maybe they would have taken them more seriously. While researching this topic I also discovered that UAB's first- and second-round games are being played at, I am not making this up, the KFC Yum! Center. Putting aside the oxymoronic nature of that name, this got me wondering if the Center is situated near the Taco Bell Guacamolito! Center for the Performing Arts, and if so, are there plans to combine the two as they sometimes do with Pizza Hut?

Then Georgia State knocked off another #3 seed, Baylor. All in all, not a good start for...anyone that thinks they know anything about basketball.
