Congratulations to Super Grover, your JQMCBP XXI Champion

With Super Grover's win, now my task comes to finding just where in the heck I can procure Black & Gold Musk Flavoured Sticks. These goodies are not typically sold outside Australia because, well, they're gross. It's going to be an interesting journey finding these musk candies (mmmm....MUSK!), but a promise is a promise. Super Grover, if that is your real name (please let that be your real name), pop me an email with you mailing address and I, The Meister of Candy, will send you these disgusting confections.

We had a two-way tie for 2nd place between Todd Campbell and Kiowa Kid, just one point back at 103. For 2nd place these two are awarded the traditional prize of BIG FAT NOTHING. As there were two at 2nd place, there was no 3rd place, so no 3rd Place Crappy CD will be sent this year.

Thanks to all participants, be they human or chimpanzee, for a wild JQMCBP XXI ride. This year was a lot of fun, what with all of the upsets and that one person who decided Musk Flavoured Sticks was Just the Thing. Hope you enjoyed it.
