Losing Mascots: Let's Talk About Them!

Pointless Discussion about Losing Teams and Their Mascots

The most excellent mascot Catamount* has come up in past JQMCBP years, but this year I did a bit extra research on its origins with the University of Vermont. In the seminal 2015 Vermont Quarterly article "Catamounts in Natural Fibers and Polyester", which never once directly references natural fibers or polyester, it is revealed that Catamount was chosen in 1926 by a 138-126 margin over the choices of Tomcat, Cow, and Camel. They don't know which one got the 126 2nd place votes, but...well it's gotta be Camel, right? Catamounts is still a personal mascot favorite, but maaaaan they could have been The Vermont Camels. Regrets.

* because we needed another word, but cooler, for a mountain lion. No, not cougar. No, not puma. No, not panther. Catamount. That's the one.
