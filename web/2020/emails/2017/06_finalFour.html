Whahappened?

Because of South Carolina's surprise presence in the final, the potential outcomes in the Quest For Candy are slightly oddball. Amazingly though, the Chimp Army is fully out of it. Lucky the Chimp has Snapchatted me video of him hurling something at the camera that I do NOT think is pudding. Sorry buddy. Next year. Always next year.

With 4 teams left and 2 to the 3rd (also known as 8!) permutations left, we have 4 players left with a chance for Sweet Sweet Candy.

  • With either Oregon as champ or a South Carolina over Oregon final, Milo Anderson gets a King Size Kit Kat.
  • If South Carolina beats North Carolina in the Battle of Carolinas, or if Gonzaga beats North Carolina in the Battle of I'm Not Sure What, Steve Shwaiko takes home a coveted Big Chunky.
  • If North Carolina is champ, eyoung54 will be mailed a Twix.
  • Finally, just like Milo my main man Craig Wadzinske will get a King Size Kit Kat sent to Milwaukee (The Good Land) should Gonzaga beat Oregon in the final

As is frequently the case going into the Final Four weekend, I'm going to be traveling. Last year I made updates in the Honolulu airport over my phone with very bad wifi. This year should be slightly simpler, but I will be in Kansas City for the semifinal games and Santa Fe the night of the championship. I'll do my best to promptly send out a final tip of the hat email from The Land of Enchantment, but if there's any significant delay it's because I'm in a green chile stupor and/or my runs-out-of-my-basement web server lost its connection. Rest assured you will all, eventually, be notified of the champ.
