Eclipse News!

Greetings from Austin, Texas, where the Candymeister, Candymeister friends, Candymeister family, and approximately a gazillion people who've never heard of the Candymeister descended and assembled to witness the 2024 Total Solar Eclipse. We made our way west a few hours to Fredricksburg, chasing both extra totality and hopefully less clouds. We did not get a fully clear sky, but we did get about 30 seconds of cloudbreak during totality, which honestly made it all worth it. Here is my video of it, which is a lot like other videos you may have seen but the only video fully approved by the JQMCBP Earth Science Division.

What does this have to do with the JQMCBP? Absolutely nothing! But I'm a huge nerd and you, an entrant in this pool, by association kind of are too. SPACE! SCIENCE! CANDY!
