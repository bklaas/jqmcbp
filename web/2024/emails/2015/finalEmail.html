Another JQMCBP year is in the books, and a hearty congrats goes to Todd "Bartman" Barton. With the Duke win, Barton secured his grand prize of a probably rancid Kirby Puckett Bar. Todd, I'll need your address to send you your biohazard. Craig Wadz-za-man...ouch, oh so close. It's a testament to your excellent bracket that even after the Wisconsin loss you finished in a tie for 2nd place with Lochlain "a jumbo pack of gumballs" McGrail. Since there was a 2nd place tie there is no 3rd place CD winner, which means my album of Bagpipe Funeral Dirges will have to wait for another year.
