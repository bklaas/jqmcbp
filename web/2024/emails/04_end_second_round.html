You Cow?!? No, UCoW™

Intro to UCoW™

Greetings CandyQuesters, rolling into the Sweet Sixteen, the time has come for the least anticipated part of JQMCBP: UCoW™, pronounced as a shrill, screeching "YOU COW!". UCoW™ stands for Unweighted Chance of Winning, and has been described by some as "major buzzkill," by others as "wholly unnecessary," and by all as "objectively unwelcome". UCoW™ is a simulation of every remaining possible permutation of the bracket and, in each case, who wins JQMCBP. At the onset of the tournament, there are 2 to the 63rd power permutations or ~ 9 quintillion possible ways the bracket could happen. That's a bit much for the circa-2011 Dell Workstation* sitting in my closet to simulate, but once the bracket is reduced to the Sweet Sixteen, the number of permutations drops to 2 to the 15th power permutations, or 32,768. That I can simulate (source code)!
