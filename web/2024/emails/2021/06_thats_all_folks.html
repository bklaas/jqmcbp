The Chimp Army Medal of Service Award

Michael Wooley-Ousdahl's selection of Krembanan has taken the 1,000 Chimp Army by storm. For the entirety of JQMCBP the Army have all selected Banana Moon Pie as their candy. No more! From here and forward, the Army has announced Krembanan as their official selection.

The "We Are What We Eat Wiki", hosted on worldofcandy.fandom.com, describes Krembanan as

a layer of sweet gel and a layer of smooth candy cream. The combination is then, as with most good candy, covered in chocolate. As its name suggests, the scrumptious bar resembles the shape of a banana.

The Chimp Army responds with "YOU HAD ME AT BANANA"
