IT'S ANTEATER TIME!

The University of California system has a lot of good mascots. The Mount Rushmore of UC mascots would include the UC-Santa Cruz Banana Slugs, the UC-Santa Barbara Gauchos, the UC-Riverside Highlanders, and without a doubt the UC-Irvine Anteaters. I didn't want them to lose, but when they did, it became time to talk anteaters.

According to my sister Abby, who literally texted me this information from the UC-Irvine campus Monday: "The anteater became the mascot of UCI when 56% of the students wrote it in on their ballots (which had 4 other choices to choose from: Golden Bison, Seahawks, Road Runners, and Unicorns)."

The Smartie Pants Award

Elsie Meilinger of Michigan requested "Smarties American, not Canadian". I mean...without that specific direction to shun the candy from our Neighbors to the North, would I have road tripped it to Winnepeg to get Elsie those other, inferior, Canadian smarties? I mean...probably.

Notably, Alyssa Campbell, also of Michigan, requested Smarties (the Nestle kind), which are otherwise known as "Canadian Smarties". What is it with Michigan and specific types of Smarties?

The world-renowned website Toronto Mike clears up the difference between these Smarties, which are truly quite different. In Canada, American Smarties are known as Rockets, whereas in Canada, Smarties appear to be, basically, M&M's.

Then I learned that Canadian Smarties are ALSO not the same as BRITISH Smarties. At which point I decided that I was not at all smarter for any of this knowledge.
