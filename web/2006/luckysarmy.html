Lucky's 1000 Chimp Army


Not satisfied with his personal-best 2001 performance as his crowning JQMCBP achievement, Lucky the Chimp has assembled 1000 of the toughest, roughest, most notorious Chimps to aid in his cause. There's Dario the Chimp, wanted for extradition by the government of Panama for Produce Fraud; Fatima the Chimp, deemed 'untreatable and clearly psychopathic' by her primate psychologists at the Univerisity of Wisconsin; Diego the Chimp, implicated as the mastermind in several mass zoo escapes. The other 997 are even worse, and ALL of them are entering this year's JQMCBP (each with their own randomly generated picks), as part of Lucky's 1000 Chimp Army.

Your success against Lucky's Army will be tracked with your Darwin Score, a measure of how many of the Army are currently beating you (tie goes to the chimp). A Score of 0 defines you squarely as a member of Homo sapiens, 500 means you aren't much different than Pan troglodytes, and a score of 1000 demonstrates that you scientifically identical to Quercus bicolor.
