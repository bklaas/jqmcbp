UCOW!

It's the most wonderful time of the year! It's UCOW (pronounced, and yelled as, YOU COW!) time! The Unweighted Chance of Winning, or UCOW (You Cow!) is when I run all remaining permutations of brackets left. This is computationally insane when there are 64 teams left (9 times 10 to the 18th possible permutations). But once the tournament is down to sixteen it's down to 2^15 or 32,768 permutations. No problem! I calculate the winner or winners of each permutation, and then compile them into a huge table to show what slim-to-none chances you have left in the Quest for Candy! It's incredibly deflating!

A couple of things to point out:

  • The 1,000 Chimp Army are about as strong as I've seen in years. Lots of possibilities left for the 1,000 Chimp Army. Could this be the year?*
  • If UCOW (You Cow!) is to be believed, I personally win 0.35% of remaining possible brackets. So you're telling me there's a chance!
  • Sometimes people ask me if I'm worried that I'll need to dole out a whole bunch of candy bars if there's a multi-way tie for first. Nope, I'm not. Once again, statistical data distributions do their thing. In > 90% of possible remaining outcomes, there is a single winner. So cool!**

* My money is on: NO
** if you're a data nerd. which I am.
