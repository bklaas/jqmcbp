Whahappened?

Well then. That was a devastating weekend for anyone that thought Louisville (#2 seed, perennially go far) Villanova (returning champions) or Duke (returning uber-villains) were going deep in the tourney.

Still, only 20 players are officially in the Hall of Shame, mathematically eliminated from the competition. However, I've also run the simulations of all remaining 32,768 permutations of the game outcomes*, and there are actually more like 600 of you mathemtically eliminated. But, that leaves a few hundred of you still alive! I'm excited to say that I win in 35 of 32,768 permutations, a full 0.11%! That's 10 times better than a one in a million chance! I like my odds.

* Really. This is called the Unweighted Chance Of Winning, or UCOW**, a patented*** JQMCBP technology.

** Pronounced, and shouted: YOU COW!

*** Not really.
