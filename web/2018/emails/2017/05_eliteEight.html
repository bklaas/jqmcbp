Candy Bar of the Day

where I single out my favorite candy selections

The Top of the Reverse Alphabetical List Award goes to Velvet Fog for the choice of Zotz!, the last alphabetically amongst 869 candybar choices.

An Absolutely True Zotz! story: A few years back I was trying to find Zotz! in Minneapolis (as one is wont to do) via my phone's voice search. I couldn't get the phone to understand what I was asking for, it kept thinking I was saying something else. Google actually saves a recording of every time you do an "OK Google" voice search into your phone, and keeps a record that you can listen to later.

So I parused my records, and here are the verbatim things I said, recorded by Google as Important Data worthy of being Saved For Eternity:

OK Google, where can I get socks in Minneapolis?
OK Google, no where can I get socks in Minneapolis?
OK Google, where can I get sod in Minneapolis?
OK Google, where can I get shots in Minneapolis?
OK Google, washcloth in my pants.

Honestly, I'm not making this up. Other gems from my personal voice search history:

OK Google, "and YOU get a car" translated into Spanish
OK Google, hammerschlagen
OK Google, voocaray recipe
OK Google, Jack Handey Deep Thoughts
OK Google, REM Green Tour 1989
OK Google, Montana University's film program
OK Google, Scooby doo show mysterious girl
OK Google, just what is in Cool Whip
OK Google, why did Bradley Cooper sign on to the Limitless show?
OK Google, is the tawny frogmouth so wise?

There are also about a half dozen times where I ask for images of mudskippers. So apparently I really like those creatures.
