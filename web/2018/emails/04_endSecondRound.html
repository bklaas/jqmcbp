Whose champion bit it?

When you fall, I will be there to catch you.
-The Floor

This section is definitely the lede. The first four days blew up a massive number of JQMCBP brackets. Virginia, the most popular pick to win it all, devestated 170 candy seekers on Friday. That's gotta be it, right? Nope.

Michigan State was only a 3-seed, but was the third most popular pick in JQMCBP. 98 people picked them as their champion. Nope.

Then North Carolina, a 2-seed, got positively shellacked by Texas A&M. Thirty-eight chocolate hopefuls had them going all the way. Nope.

The Meister's selection for winning the bracket, the Cincinnati Bearcats, went up 22 points with 10 minutes left against the Nevada Wolfpack. Excellent, I thought, this one's in the bag. Nope. Nope, nope, nope. Nevada outscores Cincinnati 32-8, tying the largest comeback in tournament history, and wins by 2. Another 22 players join the what-were-they-thinking club. Me included. #sadpandaemoji

Alright, #1 seed Xavier's really good. A shoe-in, right? Nooooooooope. Wait, Xavier lost? Well of course they did.

After all was said and done, 224 of you lost your champion over the weekend, and a mind-blowing 425, more than 50% of all human entrants, over the first four days. It's really not supposed to work like that.

Losing Mascots: Let's Talk About Them!

Pointless Discussion about Losing Teams and Their Mascots

Over the weekend we lost some very interesting mascots: Volunteers, Buckeyes, Crimson Tide, Gators, Pirates, Spartans, Rams, Bearcats, Musketeers, and a Chesapeake Bay Retriever named True Grit. But my favorite of the losing mascots has to be The Thundering Herd of Marshall. It's so non-specific, yet utterly terrifying!

"Here comes the Tigers on one side of the field and...OMG A GIANT THUNDERING HERD IS COMING FOR THE TIGERS! THERE'S NOTHING TO STOP IT! WHAT ARE THEY? I DON'T KNOW! BUT BY GOSH, IT'S THUNDERING! OH THE HUMANITY!"

It's really hard to compete with a Thundering Herd. Who could beat that? Perhaps my new For-Profit University, the Johnny Quest Library, Mines and Ophthalmology University (JQ LMaO U). Our mascot is The Field Covered in Banana Peels. You scoff at its absurdity, but how else would you stop a Thundering Herd? GO PEELS!!
