Wha' Happened?

Going in deep on some of the "games"

The Second Round had action across all the regions, 16 "games" in total. Here's the completely unnecessary details:

Viral Region

BBC Kid vs. Stuff Summer

Piper will get no solace from this, but I actually had BBC Kid penciled in for several minutes before I decided I just couldn't eliminate Stuff Summer, one of my favorite clips of all time. Stuff Summer needs to be acknolwedged! STUFF SUMMER WINS!

Duck Army vs. Gangnam Style

My first year of college, 1989-1990, I attended The Evergreen State College in Olympia, Washington. There was a store in Olympia called Yard Birds. It was the coolest. You could get anything at Yard Birds. It was like a combination Army Surplus/Fleet Farm/TJ Maxx/Home Depot. Once I got a pair of thick wool cargo pants and an engineer's hat for like $8. My friend bought a pair of slip-on loafers with palm trees on them and wore them playing basketball for the school team. I am not making this up. There was nothing you couldn't get, at steep discount, at a Yard Birds. I just googled it and the only thing that appears left of it is a site dedicated to its history. The Olympia store closed in 1993. Why am I telling you this? Because the Duck Army was exactly the kind of thing that would be available at Yard Birds. It's tough to let go of the Duck Army, or Yard Birds for that matter, but in the end the 3.5 Billion YouTube views of Gangnam Style was just too much. GANGNAM STYLE WINS!

Food Region

Tacos vs. Cheese Curds

Cheese curds are really yummy, but could you eat cheese curds for dinner 7 nights in a row? Unlikely, especially since my pocket defibrilator ran out of batteries. But tacos, sign me up! TACOS WIN!

Peanut Butter vs. Bacon

This one was too hard to pick, so I had to coin flip this one with my trusty 1926 Liberty Dollar. YouTube has informed me the video of me flipping that coin and revealing the winner has, as of typing this, "Zero Views". So, it's clear this was a big deal to all y'all. PEANUT BUTTER WINS!

Piper's Picks Region

Harry Styles vs. The Office

While The Office is known by many as a very funny sit-com of the 2000s, it also included a spooky and macabre storyline, The Scranton Strangler. Many efforts have been made to reveal the identity, some...not so good.

Most theorists have arrived at the conclusion that Toby Flenderson was the Scranton Strangler, but this year's JQMCBP has proven otherwise: Harry Styles IS the Scranton Strangler. Take his initials, "H.S.". I've learned now from several sources, all of them forwarded emails with subjects like "fwd: fwd: fwd: fwd: fwd: H.S. GUILTY PLEASE FORWARD", that Scranton was named during colonial days with the British-style city name "Hampton-upon-Scranton". Harry Styles is British. "H.S." is the initials for Hampton-upon-Scranton Strangler. Coincidence? I think not.

Sure, the show was being aired when Harry was what, about three years old, but if you completely ignore that fact the evidence is clearly there. HARRY STYLES WINS (and will live to murder again)!

Volleyball vs. Sushi

I'm guessing Piper had a tough time deciding on this one. She really does love volleyball, but in these sequestered times she's resorted to hosting backyard volleyball academy, which is Not the Same. Perhaps out of her displeasure at not being able to play with her club team any more, this gives some hint as to why she chose sushi*. SUSHI WINS!

* I mean, I wish I could ask her why she chose Sushi over Volleyball, but she'd just glare at me, silently mouthing "IT WAS MY YEAR. IT. WAS. MY. YEAR."

The grasshopper mouse eats not only other mice, but scorpions. DO NOT BE FOOLED BY ITS CUTENESS. It's seriously odd, seriously awesome. As Lance "Butterfinger" Van Houten would say, SERIOUSLY SNARFY! TARSIER WINS!

Punk Birds Region

Shrike vs. Australian Magpie

Shrikes are amazing. They're effectively a robin or a sparrow that has somehow evolved into being a murderous psycopath. They impale their prey, for later, as trophies. As a Carson-favorite Hozier put it in his (no joke here) mezmerizingly beautiful song Shrike, "Remember me, love, when I'm reborn, as the shrike to your sharp and glorious thorn." Dude. That is DARK.

Anyway, Shrikes are great. Nevertheless, I must remind you once again: THE EYES DON'T WORK THE EYES DON'T WORK GET MUM GET MUM THE EYES DON'T WORK. MAGPIE WINS!

Greater Roadrunner vs. American Dipper

Another tough one, as I've long been a fan of the glorious Water Ouzel aka American Dipper. Like the Shrike, the Dipper is a songbird that adapted to do something entirely not-songbird-like. In the Dipper's case, it's diving in fast-moving mountain streams. Pretty great.

Nevertheless, the Dipper faced the Greater Roadrunner. This large, ground-dwelling cuckoo is known not only for running really fast but also for taking down rattlesnakes. I mean, that is hardcore. ROADRUNNER WINS!

Stephen Bradbury's miracle run to Gold Medal in the 2002 Olympics, 2 years before Carson was born, has clearly resonated with here. Bradbury is into the sweet 16 with a victory over Kolton Wong's crazy double play highlight. BRADBURY WINS!
