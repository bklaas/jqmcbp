I Want Candy

It just so happens I work just a couple blocks from the Mothership, aka USBank Stadium, host of this year's Final Four. And today, thanks to the keen eyes of my lovely wife Wendy Volkman, I was alerted to the "Reese's Final Four Friday", an open-to-the-public open practice featuring the four Final Four teams as well as other events like the Reese's 3-point competition, the Reese's throw-a-foam-basketball-at-a-nice-ladies-head, the Reese's FREE CANDY BARS FOR EVERYONE, and the Texas Tech Raider Stares Ominously (not brought to you by Reese's).

This event was like the definition of Basketball-Candy intersectionality, and it would have been shameful for The Candymeister to miss it.

Wendy and I weren't the only JQMCBPers in the house either. Long-time Candy Seeker and "journalist" John Niyo was there, but he couldn't meet us on account of being "at the suite level", where he was "interviewing the Texas Tech players", or "whatever". I tried to use my fake Press Pass (Abe Saperstein, Ypsilanti Courier), but I couldn't get past the gate.
