Your 2019 JQMCBP Champion!

Tamara "Vosges Missing Link Chocolate Bar" Fink

You know, in a game like we had tonight, there can be no losers. Well except for Texas Tech. They lost. And Jacob W[adzinske], who came oh so very very close, but lost. Oh, and 836 other humans, they lost too. And 1,0001 chimps, also losers. So, actually mostly losers. My bad.

But we did have a winner! Tamara "Vosges Missing Link Chocolate Bar" Fink of Ohio fought off the other 59 that had Virginia as their champion, as well as Jacob, who would have grabbed some Jelly Bellys had Texas Tech pulled it out (Jacob still finished a respectable 10th). Tamara finished with a final score of 120, which is about 10 points higher than a typical JQMCBP winner.

Paul Petrous finished 4 points back at 116, and for finishing 2nd place Paul will receive the traditional prize of BIG FAT NOTHING.

Finally, Mark Dyal of Texas finished alone in 3rd place at 111 points, and has won a Compact Disc Recording of My Choosing.

Tamara, Mark, I'll need your mailing addresses (also Tamara, read down a bit, I need some candy clarification). Everyone else, no need to send me a mailing address, because you're all big fat losers.

Thanks for taking part in another silly year of Candy Seeking, hope you all enjoyed yourselves as much as I did. If you're overwhelmed with gratitude for your JQMCBP experience, while JQMCBP is always and forever 100% free, you may donate buckets of cash to my MS-150 charity bike ride here (I'm just a few hundred dollars from reaching the $1,000 mark, which means access to the coveted PRIORITY TOILETS). Thanks to so many of you that have already done so in contributing to my ride.

See you all next year!
