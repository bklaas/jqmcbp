The Chimps Long Slow Slide into Randomness

This is going to be a quick one as I was out for the second night in a row until 2:30 a.m. for no particularly good reason. Honestly, I am way too old for that kind of behavior. Friday night I went to this place called the Riddle Room with a gaggle of old college friends. The Riddle Room is a place where, I am not making this up, you are given you no instructions whatsoever, locked into a room that would make an OSHA inspector lose sleep, and told "You have one hour. GO!". And YOU pay THEM!* We solved it in 40 minutes though**, thankyouverymuch, which gave us a solid 20 minutes to discuss building codes and safety violations. Then we had tacos.

In the depths of head-shaking over Iowa State's loss Thursday, I completely neglected to go into detail of the brightest shining moment of the tournament thus far, the COMPLETELY BONKERS finish to the Georgia State - Baylor game. If you haven't seen the ending, it's a superb 12 seconds to watch. The best though, THE BEST, is the Premiere Coach's Reaction To A Last Second Win Ever. This coach just a week previous had torn his achilles tendon celebrating their Sun Belt Conference tournament championship, so he had to come up with a better way to show his enthusiasm. He did. Even the Georgia State Golf Team was REALLY excited about this win. So, if you're favorite team is already vanquished****, you've got a new squad to root for.

The Friday games were soooo much less interesting than the one described above, and it may have been the least eventful in recent JQMCBP memory. Very few (4) players lost their champions, almost all of the favorites won, and as a result the first day conquest of the 1000 Chimp Army, led by Julien the Chimp, will likely go down as a footnote***** in the JQMCBP history books.
