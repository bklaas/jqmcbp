UCOW!...what's a UCOW and why should I care?

The Unweighted Chance Of Winning or UCOW! tells you if there's still a chance.

Oh, I'm glad you asked! Heading into the tournament there are > 9 quintillion permutations (2 to the 63rd power) of how the bracket might play out, but after just two rounds that number drops to JUST 32,768 (2 to the 15th power). Two to the fifteenth? That's child's play! UCOW! is a thing I came up with about 10 years ago that simluates all remaining 32,768 brackets, scores them accordingly, and then reports to me, the Candymeister, how many of those remaining possibilities each of JQMCBP's participants win. Then I report them to you, so you know what your chances are, if any. Turns out, it's INCREDIBLY unpouplar! People don't like to know there's no hope. Does that stop me? Of course not. Below are the results. Ctrl-F it for your name at your own peril!

Special shout out to Hannes "Applesauce Pouch 🙂" Williams who is not only sitting in 20th place right now but also has an Unweighted Chance of Winning of ALMOST 1%. Also, by the way, in case this is relevant, Hannes is, you might not know, an infant.
